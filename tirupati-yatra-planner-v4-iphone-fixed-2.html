Road, Bus & Flight Options

Road via NH44
🚗
Own Car via NH44: Banashankari → Hosur → Krishnagiri → Vellore → Chittoor → Tirupati. ~295 km, 5.5–6.5 hrs. Petrol ~₹2,530 + toll ~₹420 ≈ ₹2,950 one-way.
🚕
Outstation Cab (Ola/Uber): Door-to-door round trip ~₹10,000–₹12,000 sedan. Book Outstation option in advance.
🚌
KSRTC Airavat / APSRTC: ₹550–₹900/seat. From Majestic Bus Stand (~14 km from Banashankari). Book at ksrtc.in. Journey 6–7 hrs.
Flight BLR → TIR
✈️
BLR → Tirupati Airport (Renigunta TIR): IndiGo / Air India Express / SpiceJet. ~55 min. ₹2,500–₹7,500 typical. 1–3 daily flights. Book 3+ weeks ahead.
📍
From Banashankari to SMVB: Auto/cab ₹120–160 (~12 km, 35 min). Allow 45 min buffer. BLR Airport to Banashankari: ~35 km, allow 2.5 hrs.

Essential Pilgrim Dos & Don'ts

✅ DO
📋
Book Special Darshan 90 days ahead — ttdsevaonline.com
📄
Carry Aadhaar card — mandatory for all darshan entry
💧
Carry water — queues last 2–10 hours
✂️
Head tonsure (Mottai) at TTD complex: ₹50. Auspicious.
🕓
Reach TTD bus stand by 4:30 AM for morning darshan
💰
Carry ₹2,000–₹3,000 cash — limited ATMs on Tirumala Hill
👗
Dress modestly — saree / salwar for women, dhoti / trousers for men
❌ DON'T
📵
Mobile phones NOT allowed inside main sanctum
👜
No leather items (belt / wallet) inside temple
🚫
Non-Hindus not permitted inside the Sanctum Sanctorum
🥩
No non-veg food anywhere in the Tirumala area
💸
Don't buy Laddoo outside — TTD counters ONLY (max 10/person @ ₹50)
⏰
Vacate TTD rooms within 24 hrs — 200% penalty after 48 hrs
